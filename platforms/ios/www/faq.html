Frequently Asked Questions

Is my information safe?
Your information is safe. All network traffic to login and submit your order goes over SSL (Secure Sockets Layer). That means there isn't any way to grab your information over the internet.

Why is there a charge from J&K Media Solutions on my credit card bill?
The charge is there because the restaurant isn't using their merchant account. The merchant account is provided as a service to the restauarant to help keep costs down on their end.

What if I forget my password?
If you forget your password, you will be emailed a new password that you can change at your leisure if you decide you want to.
